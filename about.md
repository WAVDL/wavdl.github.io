# About me

For my day job, I'm a software engineer. I graduated from The Ohio State University in 2017, and I've worked for a variety of large companies. My opinions here do not represent any of my current or former employers. If you're a potential future employer, you may be more interested in my [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/william-van-der-laar/) page.

I've lived in Columbus, Baltimore, San Francisco, and now New York City. I've been living car-free since July 2021. I take pride in moving through my city without creating unnecessary congestion, pollution, or traffic violence. I believe that a better world is possible, and I believe in creating that world wherever possible in my own lifestyle and in my community.
